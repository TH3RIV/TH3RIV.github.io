WHAT IS MUNI?

Undeterred by even the most dangerous and threatening of obstacles, Muni scouts the world with boundless enthusiasm and a cheerful spirit. A yordle with an unwavering sense of morality, she takes pride in following the Bandle Scout's Code, sometimes with such eagerness that she is unaware of the broader consequences of her actions. Though some say the existence of the Scouts is questionable, one thing is for certain: Muni's conviction is nothing to be trifled with. Muni is a legend among her yordle brothers and sisters in Bandle City. As far as yordles are concerned, there is something just slightly off about her. While Muni enjoys the companionship of other yordles, she also insists on frequent solo missions in the ongoing defense of Bandle City.

Despite her genuinely warm personality, something switches off inside Muni's mind during combat so that the lives she must end while on patrol do not burden her. Even as a young recruit, the drill instructors and other trainees found it a little disconcerting that, while Muni was normally charming and kind, she turned deadly serious and highly efficient the minute combat exercises began. While most yordles do not handle solo scouting missions with a great deal of finesse, Muni is remarkably efficient at them. Her record of success in defending Bandle City from infiltrators easily makes her one of the most dangerous yordles alive, though you'd never know it by having a cup of honey mead with her at her favorite inn. Her signature weapon - a blowgun - uses a rare ajunta poison she personally gathers from the jungles of Kumungu. To help cope with her lengthy periods of isolation, Muni recently struck up a friendship with Tristana, a fellow member of Bandle City's Special Forces. Muni is a pint-sized foe that many have come to fear and whose small size belies her fearsome resolve.
